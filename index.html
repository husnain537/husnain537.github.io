Walmart Store Sales Performance Analysis

Comprehensive sales analysis uncovering key drivers of store performance

65K+ Rows Analyzed
52% Sales from Holidays
0.75 Efficiency Correlation

Business Problem

Identify why certain stores outperform others and assess how holiday seasons influence weekly sales patterns to provide actionable recommendations for optimization.

Approach & Methodology

Applied Advanced Excel techniques including Pivot Tables, XLOOKUP functions, and statistical analysis to process 65,000+ rows of sales data. Conducted hypothesis testing to validate findings and quantify holiday effects.

Key Finding 1: Holiday Impact

Holiday periods drove 52% of total sales with strong spikes in December 2022 & 2023, indicating critical seasonal opportunities.

Key Finding 2: Store Efficiency

Strong positive correlation (0.75) between Sales and Sales per Sq. Ft reveals store efficiency as a primary revenue driver.

Key Finding 3: External Factors

Minimal correlation found with external factors (unemployment: -0.0469, temperature: -0.028, fuel: 0.007), confirming internal factors are more impactful.

Tools & Technologies

Advanced Excel Pivot Tables XLOOKUP Statistical Analysis Data Cleaning Correlation Analysis

Recommendations & Business Impact

  • Strengthen holiday promotions in low-performing stores to match top performers
  • Improve seasonal inventory planning based on December sales patterns
  • Focus optimization efforts on weaker store types and departments
  • Implement store efficiency metrics as key performance indicators
