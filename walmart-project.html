Walmart Store Sales Performance Analysis

Comprehensive analysis of 65K+ sales records to identify performance drivers and quantify holiday impact on store revenue.

Project Date
November 2024
Category
Retail Analytics
Tools Used
Advanced Excel, Statistics

Project Overview

This comprehensive analysis was conducted to understand Walmart store performance variations and identify key factors influencing sales. The project involved analyzing over 65,000 rows of sales data across multiple stores, departments, and time periods to provide actionable insights for business optimization.

Business Problem

Walmart management needed to understand why certain stores consistently outperformed others and quantify the impact of holiday seasons on weekly sales patterns. The goal was to provide data-driven recommendations for improving underperforming stores and optimizing holiday season strategies.

Key Insights & Findings

52% Sales from Holiday Periods
0.75 Efficiency Correlation
65K+ Rows Analyzed
Dec '22-'23 Peak Sales Period

Holiday Sales Impact Analysis

Holiday periods were found to drive 52% of total annual sales, with particularly strong spikes observed in December 2022 and 2023. This indicates that holiday seasons represent critical opportunities for revenue generation that should be strategically planned for.

Store Efficiency Correlation

A strong positive correlation of 0.75 was identified between Sales and Sales per Square Foot. This reveals that store efficiency is a primary driver of revenue, suggesting that optimizing store layout and operations could significantly impact overall performance.

External Factor Analysis

External economic factors showed minimal impact on sales:

  • Unemployment correlation: -0.0469 (negligible negative effect)
  • Temperature correlation: -0.028 (minimal effect)
  • Fuel price correlation: 0.007 (virtually no effect)
These findings confirm that internal store factors are more impactful than external economic conditions for Walmart's sales performance.

Methodology & Approach

The analysis was conducted using Advanced Excel techniques including:

  • Pivot Tables: For multi-dimensional data aggregation and trend analysis
  • XLOOKUP Functions: For efficient data matching and consolidation
  • Statistical Analysis: Correlation studies and hypothesis testing
  • Data Cleaning: Processing and validation of 65,000+ data points
  • Visualization: Creation of charts and graphs for pattern identification

Tools & Technologies

Advanced Excel Pivot Tables XLOOKUP Statistical Analysis Data Cleaning Correlation Analysis Data Visualization Hypothesis Testing

Business Recommendations

Strategic Actions Based on Findings

  1. Holiday Strategy Enhancement: Strengthen holiday promotions and inventory planning in low-performing stores to match top performers' success rates
  2. Efficiency Optimization: Implement store efficiency metrics as key performance indicators and focus on layout improvements
  3. Seasonal Planning: Develop targeted December sales strategies based on identified peak patterns
  4. Department Focus: Allocate resources to weaker departments identified in the analysis
  5. Performance Monitoring: Establish regular performance tracking based on the identified key metrics

Impact & Value Delivered

This analysis provided Walmart management with:

  • Clear identification of high-performing store characteristics
  • Quantified holiday season impact on revenue
  • Data-driven strategies for improving underperforming locations
  • Framework for ongoing performance monitoring
  • Actionable insights for seasonal planning and resource allocation
